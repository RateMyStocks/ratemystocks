About Us


Who We Are

ratemystocks.com is a community driven stock market research platform and investor social network founded in 2021. Our mission is bring investors, traders, and analysts of all experience levels together so that they may learn from one another as well as share opinions and strategies.

What We Offer

Community-Driven Stock/ETF Analysis

We all have the common goal of becoming better investors, analysts, & traders. At ratemystocks.com, you can view user stock ratings as well as set your own. Discuss your favorite stocks & ETFs with other users around the world. Share your opinion, strategies, and export knowledge with a great community!

Portfolio Insights & Feedback

Easily create and share sample stock portfolios and receive feedback from investors around the world. Our user interface will provide insights into your holdings, displaying both real-time & historical stock data. For inspiration, users can search for portfolios that match their investing strategy. Our platform allows users to provide feedback on other users' portfolios through comments or rating.
